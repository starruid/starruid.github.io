Glitch Art

Tool(s): Adobe Photoshop, Notepad++

Project Description: Using a doodle I had made, I took an image into Notepad and tampered with the code until the original image was glitched. I then took the glitched image, along with the original, into photoshop. There, I collaged the images together to create the final piece.
